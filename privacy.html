Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 July 2026

Everglow Cleaners Limited ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect through this website, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights under UK data protection law (the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018).

Who we are

Everglow Cleaners Limited, a company registered in England & Wales (Company No. 16001320), registered office at Novus Business Centre, Judson Road, North West Industrial Estate, Peterlee, SR8 2QJ. You can contact us at info@everglowcleanerslimited.co.uk or 07459 503353.

What data we collect

Enquiry / quote form. When you submit our contact form, we collect your full name, organisation, email address, phone number (optional), sector, and any message or site details you provide. This is sent via Formspree, a third-party form-processing service, and forwarded to our email inbox.

Website analytics. We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use this site — pages viewed, time on site, general location (country/region level), and device type. Google Analytics uses cookies to do this. This data is anonymised/aggregated and is not used to identify you personally.

WhatsApp contact. If you click our WhatsApp button, you'll be taken to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. Any conversation that follows is subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy, not this one.

Why we collect it

We use your enquiry details solely to respond to your quote request or question, arrange a site visit, and — if you go ahead — to set up and manage a cleaning contract with you. We use analytics data to understand and improve how this website performs. We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

Legal basis for processing

We process enquiry data on the basis of legitimate interest (responding to a business enquiry you've initiated) and, where a contract follows, for the performance of that contract. Analytics cookies are used with your consent, where required.

How long we keep it

Enquiry data is kept for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your enquiry and, if a contract results, for the duration of that contract plus any period required for accounting, tax, or legal purposes. If your enquiry doesn't lead to a contract, we aim to delete your details within 12 months.

Who we share it with

We share data only with the service providers needed to run this website and respond to enquiries: Formspree (form submissions), Google (analytics), and Netlify (website hosting). We do not share your data with any other third party for marketing purposes.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict how we use your data
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email info@everglowcleanerslimited.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you feel your data hasn't been handled correctly.

Cookies

This site uses cookies set by Google Analytics to measure site usage. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Blocking cookies won't affect your ability to browse this site or submit an enquiry.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

This policy is provided as a general guide and does not constitute legal advice. Everglow Cleaners Limited recommends having this policy reviewed by a qualified solicitor to ensure it fully reflects your specific data practices and complies with current law.
